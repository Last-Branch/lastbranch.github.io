About the Project

Scribit aims to develop an innovative solution for real-time Sign Language Recognition (SLR). By leveraging multimodal AI techniques, the system interprets hand gestures and facial expressions to facilitate communication between sign language users and non-signers.

The project focuses on creating a robust pipeline that is accessible and efficient, addressing substantial practical problems in assistive technology. This platform serves as the central hub for our documentation, research analysis, and system architecture designs.
